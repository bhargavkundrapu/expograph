# Module 10: Placement Toolkit

> Everything you need to land the job.

## What You'll Learn
This module teaches you to rewrite your resume for ATS, optimize LinkedIn, prepare HR answers in STAR format, practice technical interviews, and write portfolio project stories.

## Why This Matters
This is where everything comes together. All the prompting skills you learned now get applied to the one thing that matters most: getting placed.

## Lessons

| # | Lesson | What You'll Master |
|---|--------|--------------------|
| M10-L1 | ATS Resume Rewrite | Rewrite your resume to pass ATS filters |
| M10-L2 | LinkedIn Profile Upgrade | Optimize headline, about, and experience |
| M10-L3 | HR Interview Master | Prepare STAR-format answers for HR questions |
| M10-L4 | Technical Interview Practice | Generate coding + theory questions by role |
| M10-L5 | Portfolio Project Storytelling | Write project descriptions that impress |

## Key Skill
ATS optimization + STAR method. These two techniques alone can double your interview callback rate.

- Rewrite your resume and get more interview calls
- Prepare for HR and technical rounds with AI
