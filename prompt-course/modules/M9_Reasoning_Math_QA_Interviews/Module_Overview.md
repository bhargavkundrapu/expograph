# Module 9: Reasoning, Math, QA & Interviews

> Make AI think step by step. Ace interviews.

## What You'll Learn
This module teaches you step-wise reasoning prompts, controlling when AI shows work vs just answers, generating practice questions, simulating interviews, and grading answers.

## Why This Matters
Interviews, exams, and problem-solving all require structured thinking. AI can be your 24/7 practice partner - but only if you prompt it correctly.

## Lessons

| # | Lesson | What You'll Master |
|---|--------|--------------------|
| M9-L1 | Step-wise Reasoning | Force AI to show its work and think logically |
| M9-L2 | Final Only vs Show Steps | Control reasoning visibility |
| M9-L3 | Practice Question Generator | Generate unlimited practice questions |
| M9-L4 | Interview Simulator | Simulate real interview rounds |
| M9-L5 | Answer Evaluator & Rubric | Make AI grade your answers |

## Key Skill
Chain-of-thought prompting. Force AI to think step by step and you'll get dramatically better answers for math, logic, and interviews.

## Time to Complete
50-75 minutes (5 lessons x 10-15 min each)

- Practice unlimited interview questions
- Solve math and reasoning problems step by step
